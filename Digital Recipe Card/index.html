🍝 Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt, pepper, herbs

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti as per instructions.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic, then add ground beef.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and seasonings.
  4. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over pasta and enjoy!

👨‍🍳 Cook Time: 30 min | Servings: 2
